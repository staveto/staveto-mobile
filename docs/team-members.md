## Team Member Management (MVP)

Manual test checklist:

1) Owner creates project and opens Team screen.
2) Owner adds member by email; member can see project and tasks.
3) Member can update task status/notes, but cannot delete tasks, change title, change assignedTo, or edit project settings.
4) Owner removes member; member loses access immediately.
5) Contractors/Suppliers/Updates/WhatsApp webhook remain unaffected.
